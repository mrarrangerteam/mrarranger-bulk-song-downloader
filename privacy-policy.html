Privacy Policy

Extension: Mrarranger Bulk Song Downloader
Developer: Mrarranger Team
Last Updated: February 27, 2026

1. Overview

Mrarranger Bulk Song Downloader is a Chrome Extension that helps users download their own songs from Suno.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we access and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect browsing history.
  • We do not collect login credentials.
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers.
  • All processing happens locally within your browser.

3. Permissions Used

The extension requests the following permissions, used solely to enable its core functionality:

  • activeTab – To interact with the currently open Suno.com tab.
  • sidePanel – To display the downloader interface in Chrome's side panel.
  • tabs – To detect and communicate with active Suno.com tabs.
  • Host permissions (suno.com, suno.ai) – To read song metadata and download URLs from Suno's website, enabling bulk download of your own songs.

4. Data Storage

The extension does not store any personal data. Any settings (e.g., download preferences) are saved locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage and are never transmitted anywhere.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension interacts with Suno.com solely to access content that you, the user, have already created or own. We have no affiliation with Suno and do not share any data with them or any third party.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by a new "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
